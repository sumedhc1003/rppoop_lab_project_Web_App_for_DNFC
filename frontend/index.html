About Us

The fastest growing club of COEP Technological University

COEP Drama and Films(DNFC) after getiing officially registered as a club at COEP in April 2023 has been a part of continous co-curriculur growth of students which in turn resulted it in becoming the fastest growing club of COEP Tech. This is a clear outcome of the efforts taken by the team in presenting quality content which is real and should be presented to the audience. Today it has estabilished itself in par with national platforms like National Students Film Festival(NSFF) winning special jury award in it's 2024 edition.
